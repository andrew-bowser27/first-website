A New Hope: Overcoming Self Doubt and Fear

First Story by Andrew Bowser

Here we go on this journey together -- with fear and doubt. The path to finding oneself is in the areas outside of our comfort zone. Have you ever been kicked down and looked down on for nothing more than being seen as inferior? Have you ever been told that you will never amount to anything? How about labeled as something you are not? You see, sometimes it isn't other people who are doing this to you. Sometimes, it's you.

You Will Overcome!

It is difficult to stay positive when all you have ever known is negativity. Do not let anybody define who you are as a person. Discover what you are made of by exploring things you've never explored. Take chances, make mistakes, and learn. This is how growth happens. It is scary at times and especially counter intuitive to what you are used to... but that's the point! Take one step... then another... fall... get back up... repeat the process!

  1. Start Small and Start Strong
  2. Focus on RIGHT NOW!
    • Today is NOT yesterday or tomorrow!
    • Focus your energy, your being, to right now - this very moment
  3. Achievement is all in your attitude, not your environment
